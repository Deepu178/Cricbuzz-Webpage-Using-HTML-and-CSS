Commentary

Mayank Agarwal: This innings was all about grit and determination. I haven't changed much, just tried to stay side on, but it's more mental than technical. For my battingm if I don't attack I'll stuck, so if I don't put Ajaz under the pump, he will keep bowling in good areas. The Indian domestic circuit is tough, and the years anyone spends there helps them. It's hard and learnings are phenomenal, and I've carried that into international cricket. We will look to pile on as many runs as we can tomorrow and put the pressure on New Zealand.

The host didn't force the follow-on and decided fortify their lead. Pujara, batting at the top due to Gill's injuiry, was a positive in his approach. With this place under the scanner, the senior batter didn't spare any loose balls. Even fortune favoured him as New Zealand failed to review an lbw call against him when he was on 26.
